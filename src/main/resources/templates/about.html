About us

Super gym is a sports family that was born in 2022.
We are located in the beautiful city of Kharkiv, almost in the very center.
Our gym is a large assortment of the latest and most modern exercise equipment, a warm sauna and the most comfortable changing rooms.
The family includes an always friendly administrator, a highly qualified team of trainers who will find something in common with anyone and help get your body in the best shape in any condition.
Therefore, if you are looking for a sports hall for yourself and would like to get the best result and mood from visiting, we are waiting for you!
